## About Codesangam

**Codesangam** is a 2-week development challenge that brings together creativity and code. Build real-world projects, collaborate with peers, and showcase your skills to the community.

### Challenge Format

- **Duration:** 2 weeks of intensive development
- **Teams:** Solo or team participation
- **Themes:** Different themes each edition
- **Evaluation:** Based on innovation, execution, and impact

### Why Participate

- Build portfolio-worthy projects
- Learn by doing
- Get feedback from experienced developers
- Win exciting prizes

Visit [codesangam.com](https://codesangam.com) for registration and updates.
