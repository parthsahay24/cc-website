## About Weekend of Code 2026

**Weekend of Code** is the annual internal hackathon of MNNIT Allahabad, designed to kickstart the coding journey for freshers and provide a platform for seniors to showcase their development skills.

### Overview
This year, we are expecting over **500+ participants** to join us for a weekend of intense coding, learning, and fun.

### Tracks

- **DevJam** - Development Hackathon: Build innovative web, app, or system-level solutions.
- **Turing's Playground** - AI/ML Hackathon: Solve real-world problems using machine learning and AI.
- **CodeStart** - Competitive Programming: Test your algorithmic skills in an ICPC-style contest.
