## About OPC

**OPC (Online Programming Contest)** is a competitive coding event organized by the Computer Coding Club to challenge problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and coding speed. It brings together programmers of all levels to compete, learn, and push their limits in a high-energy contest environment.

### What Makes OPC Special

- **Real Competitive Experience** – Problems inspired by real CP platforms
- **High-Intensity Environment** – Time-bound challenges that test accuracy & speed
- **Open for All Skill Levels** – Beginners to advanced coders
- **Recognition & Rankings** – Performance-based leaderboard
- **Community Driven** – Connect with fellow coders through CC Club
- **One Last Battle of the Year** – End 2025 on a high note

### Tracks

1. **Competitive Programming** - Algorithms and problem solving.
2. **Logic & Analytical Thinking** - Logical and analytical reasoning
3. **Skill Building & Consistency** - Daily practice and consistency
4. **Time Management** – Solving problems accurately under strict time constraints.
3. **Coding Speed & Accuracy** - Writing correct and optimized code quickly.
3. **Real Contest Experience** - Exposure to competitive programming environments
3. **Learning Through Challenges** - Improving by tackling diverse and tricky problems.
