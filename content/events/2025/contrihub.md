## About ContriHUB

**ContriHUB** is a month-long open-source celebration where collaboration meets contribution. Organized every October alongside **Hacktoberfest**, ContriHUB aims to bring more **MNNITians** into the open-source ecosystem by encouraging meaningful contributions to real-world projects.

From fixing bugs to building new features, every pull request counts‚Äîand every contributor becomes part of a growing community of open-source enthusiasts.
